Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of oral arts to drive economic empowerment and social development in Southwest Nigeria. By providing training, resources, and support, we aim to equip young entrepreneurs with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in today's digital world while celebrating and preserving the vibrant cultural heritage of the region.

About the project


The UNESCO-IFCD-UNIOSUN Project, in partnership with the Office of the Special Adviser to the Governor of Osun State on Education and the Theatre Arts and Motion Pictures Practitioners Association of Nigeria (TAMPAN), is embarking on a groundbreaking initiative to empower young entrepreneurship through oral arts in Southwest Nigeria.

What we offer

Training Programs

We provide a range of resources and support services to help entrepreneurs turn their ideas into reality.
